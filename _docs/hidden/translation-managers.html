--- title: Translation Managers category: Hidden order: 5 ---

Our community is constantly translating DuckDuckGo into their languages. For every language, there's a large set of submitted translations, some good and some bad. To make sure that only the good ones are used on DuckDuckGo, we enlist the help of our community!

Translation Managers oversee the submitted translations in their languages. They're like moderators but for translated words, instead of forum comments.

If you'd like to become a Translation Manager, check out our page here.
